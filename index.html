About Me

Hello and Welcome!

My name is Alex Bickham, and I am a problem solver.

My entire life i've loved solving puzzles. Something about breaking down a situation and coming up with the logical solution is such an exhilirating feeling. This passion for solving puzzles evolved into a love of video games, board games, and coding alike. Video games were a wonderful mix of stories and the puzzles I was already so fond of. Whether maneuvering my way through a castle to save a Princess or exploring a new world and creating anything imaginable, video games became my creative and logical outlet.

Eventually I found the old classic, Dungeons & Dragons. It was amazing because there was so much depth to the world that seemed to bound by the paper it was written on, yet could feel so large in your mind. Creating a character and trying to work the system that was put in place was a challenge in itself, and required a great deal of imagination to make it come alive.

When I program, I picture the end goal being a finished product and each line is just another puzzle piece that you have to carefully and elegantly find it's place. There's a delicate art to it, and it takes a certain type of persistence to achieve something. Sometmes it feels like you're banging your head against a wall. Sometimes it feels like you've figure skated in the Olympics so gracefully that won the Gold Medal. It's all a balancing act. Without balance in our lives, just like our code, will become so convoluted that even you can't make sense of it. Programming is the one hobby I never get tired, because it's never the same situation. It constantly keeps you thinking and on your toes.
